# ⛳ Neon 2D Mini Golf

A vibrant, arcade-style 2D mini-golf game built entirely with vanilla HTML5, CSS3, and JavaScript. Drag, aim, and release to navigate your ball through handcrafted obstacle courses to sink your shots in the fewest strokes possible.

🚀 **Play the Live Game:** [View Mini Golf](https://ks22-bot.github.io/Mini-golf/)

---

## ✨ Features

- **Intuitive Controls:** Simple drag-and-aim mechanics that let you easily control shot power and trajectory.
- **Handcrafted Obstacles:** Navigate your golf ball through uniquely designed levels featuring complex layouts and challenging obstacles.
- **Dynamic Physics:** Smooth, simulated ball physics including bounces off walls and speed friction over grass.
- **Neon Aesthetic:** Stylized, glowing visuals that look sharp on both desktop and mobile web browsers.
- **Fully Client-Side:** Lightweight and zero-dependency, running immediately without any backend setup.

---

## 🎮 How to Play

1. **Aim & Power:** Click and hold (or tap on mobile) near the golf ball, then drag backward to change your aiming trajectory line and set your shot's power.
2. **Release:** Release the click/tap to shoot.
3. **The Goal:** Dodge the obstacles and navigate the ball into the glowing hole in as few strokes as possible!

---

## 🛠️ Built With

- **HTML5 (Canvas):** Powers the rendering of the 2D game board, obstacles, ball, and real-time trajectory graphics.
- **CSS3:** Custom page layouts and glowing arcade effects.
- **JavaScript (ES6+):** Manages game physics, collision detection, stroke tracking, and level logic.
